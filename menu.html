Menu

Choose from our freshly grilled skewers, including tender chicken, juicy lamb, and flavorful veggies. Pair your skewers with our tasty sides like seasoned rice, crispy fries, or grilled halloumi. Don’t forget to top it all off with your favorite sauce – from garlic mayo to spicy piri sauce!

Wines & Cocktails

Pair your skewers with a glass from our handpicked wine list or a fresh, vibrant cocktail. Choose from bold reds, crisp whites, or one of our signature mixes like morangoa and poncha maracuja. Ask your server for the perfect pairing!

Cheesecakes

We serve a different homemade cheesecake every evening—check today’s special! You can also order a whole custom cheesecake made just for you.
